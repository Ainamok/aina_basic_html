Lee Chong Wei

Lee Chong Wei was born in Bagan Serai, Malaysia. He initially had a passion for basketball, but was discouraged by his mother because the court got too hot. At 11 years old, Lee took up badminton after support from his father. When he was 17, Lee was drafted into the national squad. Lee won the silver medal in men’s singles at the 2008 and 2012 Olympics, falling to Chinese rival Lin Dan both times.
